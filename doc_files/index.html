Rapportsystem API Documentation

Welcome to the Rapportsystem API Documentation!

Our project, the Rapportsystem, is a sophisticated reporting solution crafted to enhance data management and analysis for "Bedrift AS," a leading manufacturer specializing in the casting of iron stoves and related products. Led by Asgeir Bjørgen Huskebakk and Rudi Holm Abrahamsen, our team is dedicated to providing a robust backend infrastructure tailored to the unique needs of industrial production.

At the core of our solution lies a powerful Flask API, meticulously designed to handle complex data processing tasks with efficiency and reliability. Security remains paramount in our design, with stringent measures such as bcrypt encryption ensuring the protection of sensitive business data.

Integrated with MariaDB, our SQL server solution offers seamless data storage and retrieval capabilities, empowering "Bedrift AS" to manage vast amounts of production data effortlessly. Additionally, our implementation of Flask-CORS facilitates smooth communication between frontend and backend systems, enabling seamless integration with existing workflows.

With a rich array of API endpoints tailored to the needs of industrial production, our solution empowers users to efficiently manage production processes, monitor equipment performance, and generate insightful reports for informed decision-making. Whether you're a developer integrating our API into your manufacturing software or a production manager leveraging our reporting capabilities, we are committed to delivering a user-friendly and scalable platform.

Thank you for choosing Rapportsystem. We are excited to partner with "Bedrift AS" on its journey towards data-driven excellence in industrial production.
